As shown by results, they all provide a diverse range of information but over the three tests, its apparent that I am a visual learner who likes to help others and is social. These attributes help me better understand what type of work I would be best in and the conditions my tasks would be best completed under. It’s another form of feedback that I will gladly take on board.

Furthermore, it seems that the results reveal I am a team player and like to put the group’s best interest above all. This is true from experience and believe I will be able to contribute well to a team environment. My job and volunteer work generally mean I work in a team often, so it is a comfortable environment. More so, when I am given the opportunity to work in a team, I like members who are prepared to put in the necessary time and effort needed to get the job done. Also, members who excel in teamwork, punctuality and above all, communication, make a team environment successful as it means there is a consistent, harmonious flow of work and the team can rely on one another and compensate for the workload when required.
